    Name: Tina Turner | Institution: Baldwin College

    "I must be frank, this is not the best program for language immersion. French isn't spoken much in daily conversations, and the Wolof class is too short to build fluency. It's great that classes and readings were in French. Without a pledge, you get what you put in for improving your language skills."

    ________________________________________________________

    Name: Leroy Brown | Institution: Neill University

    "You won't encounter many English-speakers, but if French immersion is your goal this program will only get you so far. You choose between French and Wolof, and most students choose Wolof for their internships."

    • ________________________________________________________

    Name: Leopold Senghor | Institution: University of Dakar

    "As a student from Senegal, I encourage my American peers to travel in groups at night. I do the same with my friends. There are some neighborhoods to avoid after dark, but very few problems occur during the day. Senegal is not a violent country."

    ________________________________________________________

    Name: Martha Washington | Institution: DeWitt Wallace University

    "I generally felt safe in Dakar ad always in my village. I never got over the cat-calling, though, and one of my female peers experienced attempted sexually assault. Mostly, it's important to use common sense."

    Name: Clark Gable | Institution: Carleton College
